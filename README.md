## 🚀 Projects Included

### 🛍️ 1. Customer Behavior Analysis (Data Analysis Project)
This project focuses on analyzing customer purchase behavior to understand buying patterns, customer value, and segmentation.

The goal is to extract meaningful business insights from customer transaction data.

**Key Learnings:**
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis
- Identifying high-value customers
- Business insight generation from data

**Business Insights Examples:**
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which customers are likely to stop buying?
- What products are frequently purchased together?
- How often do customers make repeat purchases?
